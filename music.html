Hot 100 Chart Methodology

The Hot 100 is Billboard's flagship singles chart, ranking the most popular songs in the United States.

Data Sources

  • Streaming data from major platforms
  • Radio airplay from monitored stations
  • Digital sales from various retailers
  • Physical sales data

Update Frequency

Chart positions are updated weekly, reflecting the previous week's activity.

Ranking Factors

Songs are ranked based on a combination of streaming, radio play, and sales data.
